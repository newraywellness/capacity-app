Inside the method

Your capacity has a color today.

Red Day · 0–35%
Critically low. Not the day to push — the day to stabilize. Survive, simplify, protect your energy.
Yellow Day · 36–70%
Enough to function, not unlimited. Make steady progress while protecting tomorrow.
Green Day · 71–100%
Resourced and capable. The day to plan, build, and grow — without spending tomorrow's energy.
